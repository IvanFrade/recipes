Anabolic Chicken & Rice!

Description

The classic bodybuilder meal. It might be basic in its ingredients, but it doesn't have to be boring! If there's something I can cook well, that's chicken and rice. It's a staple for a good reason; follow this recipe to also make it yummy!

Ingredients

  • Rice - 100g
  • Chicken - 200g
  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil - 10g
  • Spices
  • Zero-cal sauces

Steps

  1. Steam cook the rice (I use a rice cooker, but any pan will do)
  2. Cook the chicken on a non-stick pan: I usually add curry, paprika, powdered onion and garlic, black pepper and soy sauce
  3. Let both the rice and chicken cool down, add the EVO oil and mix
  4. Add zero-cal sauces as needed

Nutritional values

  • Calories: 610
  • Carbohydrates: 80
  • Protein: 45
  • Fats: 11
