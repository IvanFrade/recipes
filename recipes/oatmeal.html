Anabolic Oatmeal!

Description

The perfect way to start your morning! A chocolate-flavoured protein oatmeal with the right amount of carbs, protein and fats to kickstart your day.

Ingredients

  • Oats - 100g
  • Chocolate flavoured protein powder - 30g
  • Peanut butter - 10g
  • Milk - 250ml

Steps

  1. Bring the milk to a boil
  2. Add the oats and cook them for about 10 minutes on high heat
  3. Bring the heat down, add protein powder, creatine and peanut butter and mix well
  4. Get the porridge off the head and let it cool off

Nutritional values

  • Calories: 650
  • Carbohydrates: 70g
  • Protein: 50g
  • Fats: 17g
